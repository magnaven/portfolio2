About

Product designer with a business brain and a team-first mindset — drawn to the complex problems worth solving.

I'm an AI-forward design leader with 11+ years across B2B SaaS, health-tech, fintech, and marketplace products — with a track record of leading cross-functional teams, embedding design into commercial decision-making, and building systems that scale.

I've worked shoulder-to-shoulder with product managers and engineers to ship work that moves real metrics, and I know how to translate design vision into measurable business outcomes.

I am also part of Magna Ventures — a venture team of AI engineers, developers, and a product manager that partners with bold and driven founders to take products from concept to launch. Together, we bring the full stack of expertise needed to build and ship fast, without the overhead of a large agency.

If you're a founder ready to build something scalable, we'd love to be your team.
